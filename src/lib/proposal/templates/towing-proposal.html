The Challenge

In towing, the first truck to answer gets the job.

In roadside towing, every job goes to whoever picks up first. When you're on a hook, behind the wheel, or asleep at 2am, that call goes to the next operator on the list. Roadside callers won't wait and they won't leave a message. They just dial the next number and the job is gone before you've had a chance.

Every unanswered call is a tow that goes to the truck behind you.

Take roadside callouts 24/7 and dispatch the closest truck
TalkMate answers every call, round the clock, in your business name. It handles the callout, confirms the location, and gets your driver on the job straight away, whether it's 2pm or 2am on a Sunday.
Capture location, vehicle, and rego details on the first call
No more chasing customers for their address or rego number. TalkMate collects every detail your driver needs upfront and sends you a clean job summary the moment the call ends.
Text the customer their ETA so they stop ringing for updates
Once the job is confirmed, TalkMate sends the customer an automatic text with their estimated arrival time. Your phone stays quiet, your driver stays focused, and your customer feels looked after.

How It Works

Simple to set up. Works while you're on the road.

TalkMate plugs into your existing phone number. There's no new app, no hardware, and no complicated setup. Here's what happens on every single call.

1
A customer calls your number
Day or night, weekday or weekend. TalkMate answers on the first ring, in your business name, before the call ever hits voicemail. The customer hears a professional receptionist, not a generic recording.
2
TalkMate handles the call
It greets the caller as your receptionist, asks the right questions for a towing job, and keeps them calm and confident while it works. It knows your business, your services, and your coverage area.
3
Job details captured cleanly
Location, vehicle description, rego plate, and any special notes are collected on the spot. No back and forth, no missing info. A clean job summary is ready the moment the call ends.
4
You get the job, they get the ETA
The job summary lands as a text to you or your dispatcher instantly. The customer gets an automatic ETA text so they're not ringing back. Your phone stays quiet and the job gets done.
Works day and night, all weekend, and when every truck is already out. No sick days, no missed calls, no voicemail.
